Overview

I was a project manager and UI designer on the QuickStrike team at ProLook, leading design on the Riddell Sports Uniform Customizer. I worked closely with engineering and product managers on the client side to ensure designs aligned with company goals and the engineering roadmap. Primary focus: Select Style, Select Cut, and Customize steps.

My Role
Project Manager, UI Designer, Product Development
Team
Cross-functional (ProLook + Riddell)
Duration
Apr 2017 – Nov 2021
THE CHALLENGE

Competitors were closing deals while reps waited for revisions.

While trying to make a sale to a school, the time from leaving the coach's office to sending specs to designers — waiting for initial designs, revisions (often 8+ rounds) — took far too long. Competitors were closing deals in the time ProLook took to send a first draft. Performance issues with existing internal tools made it worse.

How might we give sales reps the ability to customize uniforms in real time with the coach — closing deals in the first meeting?

THE SOLUTION

Real-time. 3D. In the room.

Create a 3D online customizer that could be used in real time with the coach during the first meeting — allowing both to make design changes on the spot. No waiting. No email chains. No lost sales.

MY CONTRIBUTION

From wireframes to engineering handoff.

01 UI Design — Select Style, Select Cut, Customize
Led design across three core steps of the customizer flow. Created wireframes, high-fidelity designs, and annotations for engineering. Ensured alignment between visual design and technical feasibility at every stage.
02 Cross-team Collaboration
Worked directly with ProLook engineering and Riddell product managers to align design decisions with technical constraints and business goals — bridging the gap between design intent and build feasibility throughout the project.
03 User Research & Testing
Selected to produce wireframes and conduct user research and testing for the customizer — validating that designs met both project requirements and real customer needs.
IMPACT
A tool that changed how ProLook closes sales.
Real-time closing

3D accuracy
Realistic 3D view reduces revision rounds by showing the final uniform before production
Competitive edge

Reflection

This project expanded my practice beyond pure UX into product development and cross-functional project management.
